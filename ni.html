If you plan completing the challenge you should equip yourself with these necessary items

  • Boots
  • Suitable clothing
  • Maps, Compass
  • GPS device, This would need to be capable of getting you to the start of the walk and identifying the exact County High Point. It would be great if it can record your route also and of course guide you on the walk to the top.
  • The book, Ireland's County High Points A Walking Guide by Kieron Gribbon (see picture below). This great book gives details of all the County High Points, a suitable start point, some reference points for the walk and the exact location of each County High Point. Check out Kierons great websites for more walking information and tips at www.kierongribbon.com or the County High Points are listed as the County Gribbons on the High Point Ireland website
